## Versão Reddit (r/Belgium ou similares — mais técnica/contexto)

```
Title: Built a free, open-source Dutch course specifically for Brazilian Portuguese speakers moving
to Flanders

I made a free Dutch (Flemish) learning app aimed at Brazilian Portuguese speakers relocating here —
mainly because I went through the process myself and existing tools (Duolingo, Babbel) don't map
grammar between the two languages well.

The main idea: every example sentence shows Dutch and Portuguese side by side with matching word-type
color coding (verbs, nouns, articles...) so you can pattern-match structure instead of just memorizing
phrases. Covers CEFR A1 through C2, includes inburgering/NT2-relevant vocabulary (bank, housing,
healthcare, bureaucracy), spaced-repetition flashcards, and works offline as a PWA.

No signup, no ads by default, fully open source: https://nederlands-voor-brazilianen.vercel.app
Repo: https://github.com/Cloesick/nederlands-voor-brazilianen

Feedback welcome, especially from people who've actually done inburgering/NT2 here.
```
